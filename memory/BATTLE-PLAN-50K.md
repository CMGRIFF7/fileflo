## FMCSA Violation Lead Pipeline

**Source:** FMCSA Safety Measurement System (SMS) — public data
**URL:** https://ai.fmcsa.dot.gov/SMS/
**Method:**
1. Pull carriers with violations in last 90 days
2. Cross-reference DOT numbers with Apollo for contact info
3. Cold email subject: "Your recent FMCSA violation — how to prevent the next one"
4. Call within 48 hours of email

**Why this works:** They just got fined. They're embarrassed, scared, and actively looking for a solution. Conversion rate is 3-5x standard cold outreach.
